Alignment & visual flow
Center‑align hero copy, then left‑align everything below the fold. It draws eyes to the headline but lets users scan prices/forms quickly.

Sticky booking button (bottom‑right on desktop, full‑width bar on mobile) ensures conversion without hunting.

Progress indicator (steps 1‑3) sits 32 px above the form on every modal; reserve 64 px min for close button & safe‑touch zone.

Practical tips
Modal padding: 32 px desktop, 24 px tablet, 20 px mobile.

Safe‑area insets (iOS notch) add padding-bottom: env(safe-area-inset-bottom) for mobile bottom sheets.

Max modal height: calc(100vh - 120px) so the dimmed background always peeks through; scroll the internal .modal-body.

Animation: 200 ms ease‑out fade + 8 px translate‑up feels snappy and unobtrusive.
